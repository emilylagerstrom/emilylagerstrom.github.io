Basketball #34

Basketball was a huge part of my life while I was in high school. As a freshman, I made the varsity girls team. In my career, I have made many friends and memories. Our high school team has always been successful for awhile. Scroll through this page to see some of my career highlights!

In my career I have:

  • Made 169 three pointers
  • Ended with 749 career points
  • Won an Eastern Maine Championship
  • Was named a Miss Maine Basketball Semifinalist!
Advice to my readers:

The time in high school that is played doing the sports you love goes by too fast. Cherish every moment, every win, every pre game ritual, and every memory you've made with your teammates. Practicing the sport you love is most important to become successful. Even though you want to skip that day of practice, it is important that you fit time in every day to work on something that you aren't the best in. It will be rewarding.
